Date: Wed, 20 Nov 1996 19:15:53 GMT Server: Apache/1.0.3 Content-type: text/html Content-length: 1604 Last-modified: Wed, 29 Nov 1995 21:49:21 GMT Visual Design of Analog Computers Visual Design of Analog Computers Description: Analog field computers have a strongly visual component in their design. Whether the analogy is concrete (such as to a butterfly wing) or abstract (such as to a matrix of quaternions), the ability to design a field computer depends on the ability to visualize the system. Our research involves defining the transformations used to map a system to an analog computer, and formalizing the inference methods used in visual design. Associated Faculty: Jonathan W.
