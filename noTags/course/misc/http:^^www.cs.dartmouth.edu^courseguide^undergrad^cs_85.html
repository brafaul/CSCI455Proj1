Recommended prerequisite will vary with term. Consult the Department for the topic in 97W and 98W. Dist: QDS Each year a course in an advanced topic in theoretical computer science is offered. Topics covered in recent years include combinatorial optimization, computational geometry, cryptography, network flows, and distributed algorithms.
