Course Description This is a comprehensive course in project management tailored to the needs of individuals involved in the development of software or software-intensive products. Software project management deals with the methods, techniques, and tools that will help to achieve a successful product development effort, meeting cost, schedule, and quality targets. The course is intended for technical contributors, individuals wanting to become project managers, and those already performing project management duties.
