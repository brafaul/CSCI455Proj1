CSC379 title page
CSC 379: Ethics in Computing

This course is a survey of the ethical issues involved  in
computing.  It discusses the way that computers and software pose new
ethical questions or pose new versions of standard moral problems and
dilemmas. It stresses case studies that relate to ethical theory.
