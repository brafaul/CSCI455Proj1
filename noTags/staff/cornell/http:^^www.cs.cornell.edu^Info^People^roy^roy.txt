Ken Birman and

Department of Computer Science at the

Technion - Israel Institute of Technology.

Hagit Attiya, and my thesis title was

Consistency Conditions for Distributed Shared Memories.

Millipede project, working with

Department of Computer Science at the

Technion - Israel Institute of Technology.
