Wagner (High-resolution version of this picture, 1020x620) (Even bigger version) Neal R. Wagner Associate Professor Ph.D., M.A., Mathematics, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 1970. B.A., Mathematics and English, University of Kansas, 1962. Areas of Interest Cryptography, database security, randomization, and visualization. Summary of Research Current work also looks at the use of cryptography and randomization in a distributed setting, particularly solving distributed security problems using coin-flipping techniques. I am also interested in visualization, with a current project to visualize Internet news.
