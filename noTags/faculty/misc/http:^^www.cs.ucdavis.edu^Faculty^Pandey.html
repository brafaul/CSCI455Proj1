His current focus of research is on design and implementation of concurrent and distributed object-oriented programming languages. The research includes development of novel techniques for implementation of execution environments for concurrent, distributed, and mobile programs. Professor Pandey is also conducting research on software design and development techniques for parallel and distributed applications. Click here to see Dr. Pandey's personal web page.
