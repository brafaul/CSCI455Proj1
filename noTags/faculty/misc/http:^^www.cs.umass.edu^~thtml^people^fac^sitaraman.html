Sitaraman Ramesh Kumar Sitaraman Prof. Ramesh Sitaraman received his B. Tech. in electrical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. He received his Ph.D. in computer science from Princeton University in 1993. He is currently a member of the Theoretical Computer Science group at the Computer Science Department at UMASS. His research interests are in the theory of parallel computing, performance analysis of packet routing, fault tolerance, and wireless networks. With Prof. Arnold Rosenberg, he co-directs the Theory of Parallel and Distributed Processing group.
