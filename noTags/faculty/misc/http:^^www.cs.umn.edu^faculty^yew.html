Research Interests High-performance processor architectures, parallelizing compilers, parallel machine organizations, performance evaluation, parallel discrete event-driven simulations Current Research Projects Agassiz Project: (Co-investigators: Prof. Zhiyuan Li (CS) and Prof. David Lilja (EE)) The primary goals of the project are to study architectural, compiler, and machine organizational issues for high-performance computer systems including both uniprocessors and multiprocessors that exploit medium-grained and fine-grained parallelism.
