My Doctoral Research - Descartes My doctoral dissertation research is on dynamically adaptive profile-driven program specialization. I have been building a prototype system which I call Descartes. The name is a corruption of the initials for Dynamically Adaptive Scheme Compiler And Run-Time Execution Specializer. My thesis advisor is fond of describing it as a self-aware compiler and run-time system. My thesis defense is scheduled for the end of this term (April 1996).
