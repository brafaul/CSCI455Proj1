I graduated in 1994 with a B.S. in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, NJ. Multimedia Courseware for Computer Science Education For this project, we're designing a large set of web pages (and tools for creating & organizing them) that will be a resource for an introductory graphics course. The pages aren't quite ready for public consumption, but should be soon. Educational Applications of Visualizations I'm continuing a project I started this summer with John Stasko to evaluate the use of animation in teaching algorithms.
