Now we have a event circle again -- what is the cause and what is the result? Rather confusing but I like it. Boxing Helena Stupid thing. Emma Lovely love story! Many love stories, the funny ones, the weird ones, and the happy ones. Gwyneth Paltrow, I should remember this name 'cause she will be nominated for best actress next year. Let's see. The Crow: City of Angels Impressive visual effect. With a dark warm hue and misty scenes, it created a good atmosphere of myst and evilness. Courage Under Fire Meg Ryan is a hero! Is her funny face suitable for a role like this? Yet this face did bring much humanity and liveliness to the role. It's an old trick of different people telling different stories. The plot was nothing original. A Time To Kill A black man killed two sons of bitches who raped his little daughter. He is innocent by our good wills but shouldn't be so by law. Nonsense. Bogus What is more beautiful and innocent than a child's imagination? Try to wake up yours and see your Bogus! Il Postino The BEST I have seen since I came to the States. Ordinary people's ordinary stories. The simpliest life and the simpliest feelings with the most glaring part of humanity. After all the Hollywood cliches, I felt like breathing fresh air when I watched this movie. Bravo! Fly Away Home Just okay. Lone Star Looks like a low-budget movie. Yet the way the director mixed up the past and the present was remarkable and interesting. Tombstone Another Wyatt Earp story. I saw Kevin Costner's first but I don't know which is made first. Anyway, this one seems better than the overlong Costner one. The Three Muskiteers Duma's story itself was just an entertainment story so I don't expect more from this movie. A good comedy and not bad as a Tuesday Movie. Gentleman's Agreement Perhaps it's too old for me to like it. Maybe the old fashioned acting doesn't attract me any more? Rocky Certainly better than the later Rockys. The ending made the motif clear: What's more important to an ordinary man like Rocky? It's not winning the game. Although he had tried so hard to win. At the last moment, he didn't care about the result of the game. He only wanted to say "I love you" to his girlfriend. Three Colors: White White has a clearer plot than the other two although it is just the same weird. I still prefer Red among the three which is the first one I saw. Terms Of Endearments It was only the final part that touched me somehow. Gandhi Not good as a movie but as a historical fact. Ben Kinsley was great playing Gandhi from young to old. The Island of Dr. Moreau Cool. This is always my favorite type. The plot was just a combination of Jurassic Park and Frankenstein but was still thrilling. The most remarkable thing is not the story itself but the issue it raised: what will become after man is able to create human? It will be inevitable to develop such a technology in near future. As the hyena-man asked, "what am I?" Dr. Moreau couldn't answer. Can anybody answer? Marlon Brando is perhaps the best actor to play such god-like role. After his Commander Kurtz in Apocalypse Now and Don Corleone in The Godfather, this Dr. Moreau was not difficult for him. Another actor is noteworthy, who played the eyewitness of the island. David Thewlis, whom I didn't know before, acted perfectly as an anti-hero in this film. The French Connection Not bad. The chasing scene was outstanding. Even now in '90s, they still seldom make things better than that. Blade Runner Said to be classical for its visual effect. Maybe it is the only thing that is not bad. I felt sick. The Mirror Has Two Faces Very Nice. Like Emma, it is a romantic comedy. Healthy humor. Looks like a low budget movie but the stupid thing is the microphone appeared in the screen! The plot isn't perfect but the acting was grrrrreat! Back to my homepage. Send me your comments. LAST MODIFIED: Dec 8, 1996
