My dissertation research specifically addressed the development and application of adaptive memory strategies for the solution of jobshop and FMS production scheduling problems using Tabu Search. Work in this area is continuing. Additional interests include software engineering, especially lifecycle strategies and the application of object oriented methods; management and applications of distributed information resources, including nets, webs, and EDI/EC issues; and the management of enterprise information technology and facilities.
