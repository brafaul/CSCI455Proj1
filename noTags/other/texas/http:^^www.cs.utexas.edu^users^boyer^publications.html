Software System: The Boyer-Moore theorem-proving system (also known as Nqthm) may be obtained by anonymous ftp from Internet site ftp.cli.com. The most recent release of this program occurred in January, 1994. To obtain a copy, fetch the file /pub/nqthm/nqthm-1992/README and follow the directions therein. The Common Lisp sources for the system are included in the release. The most recent release includes many mechanically-checked theorems formulated by Bevier, Boyer, Brock, Bronstein, Cowles, Flatau, Hunt, Kaufmann, Kunen, Moore, Nagayama, Russinoff, Shankar, Talcott, Wilding, Yu, and others. Altogether, the the most recent release, including the examples and documentation, comprises approximately 15 megabytes. Running the theorem-prover on these examples generates approximately 140 megabytes of readable proofs. An alternative site for the sources is here . Back to Boyer's web page.
