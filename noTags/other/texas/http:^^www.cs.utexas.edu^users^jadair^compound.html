The group is centered around a house with high ceilings, hardwood floors, terrible plumbing, and many hidden nooks and crannies which could conceivably be filled with butter. Once, three separate orders for pizza were placed on one night from the Compound. Any alleged relationship between the Compound and the NSA is purely speculative and will be vigorously denied (as will the existence of the NSA). Compound residents: J.P.
