MIME-Version: 1.0 Server: CERN/3.0 Date: Monday, 06-Jan-97 22:52:33 GMT Content-Type: text/html Content-Length: 4844 Last-Modified: Monday, 28-Oct-96 03:12:35 GMT Ken Harker - Babylon 5 Ken Harker - Babylon 5 Babylon 5 is really the only television show that I watch regularly. It is beyond a doubt one of the highest quality shows on the air. The show is unique among the offerings currently on television, in that it is planned along a five year "story arc" in which each episode, while standing on its own, contributes to the big picture. Babylon 5 has already won two Emmy Awards for its high quality production, and has been nominated for several more. The special effects on the show are incredible, literally setting the standards by which to judge other shows!
