Date: Mon, 11 Nov 1996 17:04:24 GMT Server: NCSA/1.5 Content-type: text/html Last-modified: Sun, 01 Sep 1996 04:00:54 GMT Content-length: 1086 CS 302 Fall 1996 - Section 2 Late Policy CS 302 Fall 1996 - Section 2 Late Policy To be on time, your paper copy of the program must be handed in by the BEGINNING of class on the day it is due. You will be allowed to turn ONE program up to one class period late with no penalty on the condition that you send me an E-mail message at least 48 hours before the deadline. You may use this extension for any reason (e.g. the Packers were playing the Lions) but I highly recommend that you wait until you really need it. You will be allowed to turn THREE programs up to one class period late with a twenty percent (20%) penalty. After this quota is met, all late programs will be accepted only under extreme circumstances (e.g. severe illness, death) and require 48 hours notice prior to the set due date. Programs more than one class period late have the same policy as a fourth (or more) late program (see above).
