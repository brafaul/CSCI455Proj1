CS310 Assignment Requirements


 Turning in your homework 

When turning in homework, put your name and lab section number in
the upper right hand corner of the first page.
You will lose 5 points if you don't do this.

 Put your answers to the questions together on a separate
sheet of paper.  
 If the TA grading your paper can not easily find
your answers, you will lose points.  

 Homework is due at the end of the class period.


 No late homework is accepted.



 Grading Policy 

 The two midterms and the final are each 20% of the final grade.


 The assignment score is 40% of the final grade.


 The assignment score is the sum of the highest 7 assignment scores
out of the 8 assignments.


 Do your own work! 

Each student must do his or her own work. 
  

 It is considered cheating
to turn in all or part of someone else's work as your own. 
  

 You may discuss homework problems with other students, but you 
are not to copy anyone else's written work or code. 
  

 You must not do work for another student. 
  

 Penalties for cheating will be imposed.
