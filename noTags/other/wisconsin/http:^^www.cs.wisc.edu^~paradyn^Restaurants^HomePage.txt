Welcome to the Computer Sciences Department's Restaurant Review!

Important note: These reviews reflect the opinions of individual members of
our community and do NOT represent an official position of the Computer
Sciences Department, University of Wisconsin, State of Wisconsin, United States
of America, or any other organization or deity.


Please verify all factual data (such as hours, credit cards, etc.) by contacting
the restaurant directly.
For that matter, the best way to verify the opinions of the reviewers is to try
the restaurant yourself.

The reviews are contributed by a variety of individuals.
Each comment in the review is preceded by the initials of the person who
contributed these comments to the review.
This is a moderated document.
If you want to contributed to it, please let me know
(bpm).

for current reviews.
