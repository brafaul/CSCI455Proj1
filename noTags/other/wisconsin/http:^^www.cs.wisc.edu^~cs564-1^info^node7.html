Date: Tue, 05 Nov 1996 00:28:01 GMT Server: NCSA/1.5 Content-type: text/html Last-modified: Thu, 19 Sep 1996 18:00:05 GMT Content-length: 2262 Important Policy Issues Next: About this document Up: CS564 Fall 1996 Course Previous: Important Dates Important Policy Issues Assignments must be received by the TAs or the instructor by 5:00 PM on the day when they are due. Assignments received later will lose 5 % of the grade for each of the first three days. After three days, the solution will be made public, and assignments turned in subsequently receive no credit. In order to avoid lateness caused by machine loads, coincident due dates for CS classes, etc., simply be sure to get started right away on each assignment. Things are certain to go wrong now and then, so don't wait until the last minute to start. Please note that in a project team, each member has three distinct responsibilities: To decide upon a schedule of tasks for each assignment, together with the other partner. To complete the tasks that he or she agreed to complete. To *frequently* check on how his or her partner is progressing on their tasks. So you should not just ask your partner about where they are the day before the assignment is due! Wed Sep 6 11:38:58 CDT 1996
