 Material in Engineering Library

The Engineering Library is located at the NW corner of the
engineering quadrangle. It contains the following
books on reference, and the following journals in the
stacks. Also, you can check the on-line catalogs there,
or via the WWW.

 Reference Books 

 Korth & Silberschatz: Database System Concepts.
       McGraw-Hill, Second Edition, 1991. 

 Michael Stonebraker: Readings in Database Systems.
     Morgan Kaufmann, Second Edition, 1994.

 Elmasri & Navathe: Fundamentals of Database Systems.
       Benjamin-Cummings, Second Edition, 1994. 

 Gray & Reuter: Transaction Processing: Concepts and Techniques.
       Morgan Kaufmann, 1993.
