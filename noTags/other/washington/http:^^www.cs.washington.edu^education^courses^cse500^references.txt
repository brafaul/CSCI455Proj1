Here are some books and journals available for reference, either on reserve
in the Engineering Library or in my office.

Books and Journals in my Office

Michael Benedikt (editor), Cyberspace: First Steps.  Mit
Press, 1991.

James Brook and Iain A. Boal (editors), Resisting the Virtual Life:
The Culture and Politics of Information, City Lights, 1995.

Edward Cavazos and Gavino Morin, Cyberspace and the Law, MIT
Press, 1994.

Bill Gates, The Road Ahead, Viking, 1995.

Lance Hoffman (editor), Building in Big Brother,
Springer-Verlag, 1995.

Brian Kahin and James Keller, Public Access to the Internet,
MIT Press, 1995.

Howard Rheingod, The Virtual Community: Homesteading on the
Electronic Frontier, Harper 1993.

Clifford Stoll, Silicon Snake Oil, Doubleday, 1995.
