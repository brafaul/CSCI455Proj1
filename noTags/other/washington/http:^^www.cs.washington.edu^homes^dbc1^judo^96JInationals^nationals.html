I won my first match, but lost my second, so I had some time to snap a few pictures. I figure one of two things will happen: either I practice and get better at judo or I get more practice taking pictures. In any case, the camcorder ran out of juice filming the morning competition, so you won't be able to evaluate my judo - but the pictures are here for all to see.
