The goal of this work is to provide support of empirical AI research. I am currently working on an extension of the Buridan and C-Buridan planners. These planners are based on decision theory and incorporate a probabilistic view of planning and plan execution. Publications Anderson, Scott D., Adam Carlson, David L. Westbrook, David M.
