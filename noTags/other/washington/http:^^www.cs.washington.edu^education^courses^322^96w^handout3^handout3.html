You should have read and understood the following chapters and sections of Sudkamp. Chapter 1, all sections. Pay particular attention to to Section 1.3, Countable and Uncountable Sets. Chapter 2, all sections. Chapter 3, all sections. Chapter 4. We did not cover this chapter in detail. You should know and understand the nondeterministic top-down and bottom-up parsing methods. Chapter 5, only section 5.3, Useless Symbols. Although we did not cover this section directly, you should read and understand it. Chapter 6, all sections. We did not talk much about -NFA's, but you should know what they are. Sudkamp's approach to showing the equivalence of DFA's, NFA's, regular expressions, and regular grammars was different than mine. Nonetheless, try to read and understand his approach. Chapter 7, all sections except section 7.3, The Pumping Lemma for Regular Languages. Chapter 8, all sections except, 8.3 and 8.6. Also remember to study the DPDA too, which is not covered in the book. Chapter 9, only sections 9.1 and 9.2. Chapter 11, only sections 11.1, 11.2, 11.3, 11.4. and 11.8.
