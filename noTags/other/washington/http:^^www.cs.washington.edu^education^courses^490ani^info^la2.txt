Laboratory for Animation Arts















The Laboratory for Animation Arts (LA2) features top-of-the-line 
hardware and software for creating computer animation.  Occupying
two rooms, it features 7 animation workstations featuring SGI
Indigo2 Extremes.  


The laboratory is supported by the Center for Advanced Research Technology
in the Arts and Humanities
(CARTAH), and was founded
as a joint effort
among the School of Art, Department of Computer Science and
Engineering, and School of Music.


LA2 is equipped via industrial gifts from the following companies:

Silicon Graphics, Inc.
Alias | Wavefront
Pixar
