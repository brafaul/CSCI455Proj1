Maynard Engebretson Interests RESEARCH INTERESTS: Dr. Engebretson's interests include the application of computers and digital methods to problems of speech processing, multimedia, signal enhancement, acoustic array processing and hearing and deafness. He is currently working on audio algorithms for voice-activated switching, audio bridging, and for improving the quality of acoustic signals that are corrupted by noise and reverberation.
