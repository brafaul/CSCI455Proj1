Rutgers University Online Services



Rutgers University Online Network Services

 

The following links provide interactive information services for
Rutgers University and the Internet. 
 
WhitePages provides access to the Rutgers University Faculty,
Staff, and Student directories, and to similar directories at other
sites on the Internet. 


Archie
locates publicly available software and other information on the
Internet. This WWW gateway will let you search for and retrieve files
for use on your local computer.


Rutgers FTP servers 
offer a variety of information and software for public access. 


INFO: The Rutgers University Campus-Wide Information System, provides
information about University facilities and services.
