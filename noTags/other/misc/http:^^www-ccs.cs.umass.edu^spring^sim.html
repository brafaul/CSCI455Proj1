Date: Tue, 14 Jan 1997 21:49:02 GMT Server: NCSA/1.4.2 Content-type: text/html Last-modified: Fri, 01 Sep 1995 15:19:39 GMT Content-length: 1704 Real-Time Systems Simulators Real-Time Systems: Simulators University of Massachusetts, Amherst Spring Real-Time Scheduling The Spring real-time scheduling simulator supports the study of various real-time scheduling algorithms. This simulator integrates with the flexible manufacturing research being done in the CARTS lab. This is a multi-level simulator that deals with resources at two levels of abstraction (at the high level: products to manufacture, raw materials, etc. and at the low level: computational and data resources) and their linkage. Real-Time Active Database Experimental Simulator Real-Time Active Database Experimental Simulator (RADEx) is a real-time, active, temporal database simulator. Current Work Enhancing both the Spring scheduling simulator and the RADEx simulator top the list of current work in this area.
