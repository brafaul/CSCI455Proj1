Date: Tue, 26 Nov 1996 23:15:26 GMT Server: NCSA/1.5 Content-type: text/html Department of Computer Science, The University of Iowa The University of IowaDepartment of Computer Science The administrative offices of the Department of Computer Science at The University of Iowa are located in Room 14 of MacLean Hall. Normal hours are 8:30 a.m. to noon and 1:00 to 4:30 p.m CST/CDT, Monday through Friday, while school is in session. The current department chair is Steve Bruell. The department offers a minor in Computer Science; the undergraduate degrees of Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science; and the graduate degrees of Master of Science and Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science. Information about admissions is available from the Office of Admissions.
